Reverse Sear Ribeye Recipe

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steak
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 300
  2. Dry steaks with paper towel
  3. Season steaks to your preference
  4. Put steaks in oven on a rack with foil trap beneath
  5. Preheat to high an oiled cast iron skillet on stovetop
  6. Allow steaks to reach desired internal temp (130 suggested)
  7. Remove steaks from oven and place onto cast iron
  8. Add desired amount of butter
  9. Sear all sides for 1 minute each while bathing in melted butter
  10. Remove, rest, enjoy!
