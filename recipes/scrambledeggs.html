Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Butter
  • Milk

Directions

  1. Crack desired quantity of eggs in bowl
  2. Add splash of milk
  3. Add seasonings and cheese as desired
  4. Scramble with fork until consistent
  5. Melt butter in pan on stovetop
  6. Pour egg mixture onto hot, buttery pan
  7. Use spatula to keep the mixture moving as it solidifies
  8. Cook and move until desired consistency
  9. Plate and enjoy!
